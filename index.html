Our Story

In the heart of Italy, where tradition meets passion, Dolce Vita Cocktails was born. Our story begins with Nonna Sofia, who believed that every celebration deserved a perfect cocktail. Her secret recipes, passed down through generations, capture the essence of Italian hospitality and the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean.

Today, we honor her legacy by crafting each cocktail with the same love and attention to detail. From the sun-kissed lemons of the Amalfi Coast to the rich grapes of Tuscany, our ingredients tell a story of Italy's diverse regions. Each sip is a journey through the rolling hills, ancient cities, and charming villages that make Italy so enchanting.

Signature Cocktails

Taste the essence of Italy in every sip

Aperol Spritz

A refreshing blend of Aperol, Prosecco, and a splash of soda water. Perfect for warm summer evenings on the patio.

Classic Negroni

A sophisticated balance of gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth. A timeless Italian classic with a rich history.

Limoncello Spritz

Bright and zesty with homemade limoncello, Prosecco, and fresh mint. A taste of the Amalfi Coast in a glass.

Venetian Bellini

A delightful combination of fresh peach purée and Prosecco. Created in Venice, this cocktail is pure elegance.
